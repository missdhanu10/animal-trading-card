Deer

Deer have have a lot of muscles attached to their ears which allow them to turn their ears in any direction

  • Family:Cervidae;Goldfuss,1820
  • Scientific name:Cervidae
  • Speed:60-80km/h
  • Mass:Red deer-200 kg
  • Lifespan:Moose- 15-25 years

The sizes of deer vary as some are humongous while others are very tiny The Southern Pudu weighs about 9 KG goes as far as 14 inches tall when at full maturity making it the smallest of the family.
